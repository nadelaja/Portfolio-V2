About the Game

Order Up! is a fast-paced card game I desighed where players compete as chefs to complete recipes using ingredient cards. Through a mix of strategic resource management and quick decision-making, players try to balance the ingredients in their hand while watching out for penalty cards that could derail their progress.

Initial Version

The first iteration included trading mechanics and strict hand limits, which slow down gameplay. Early playtesting also revealed issues with recipe completion difficulty and card distribution.

Playtesting

Through several rounds of playtesting, key improvements were made:

  • Simplified recipe requirements for better game flow
  • Added wildcards to prevent gameplay stagnation
  • Introduced penalty cards for player interaction
  • Adjusted ingredient card distribution for better balance
  • Modified win conditions for more satisfying gameplay

Final Design

The final game features carefully balanced card distribution:

  • Recipe requirements ranging from 1-3 ingredients
  • 26 Recipe Cards with varying complexity
  • 56 Ingredient Cards spread across 12 different types
  • 6 Wildcard "Fully Stocked Fridge" cards that can substitute any ingredient
  • 6 Penalty Cards that force discards (One Star Review) or recipe forfeitures (Health Code Violation)

About the Game

Broker Breaker is an action-progression PC game for casual gamers aged 8 and up. Set in the of chaos Wall Street, players battle through waves of increasingly challenging enemies while managing resources and upgrading their abilities, culminating in an epic boss battle.

Core Gameplay

The game loop has players defeat enemies as they spawn spaw. They battle them, earn experince to strenghten their abilities, advance through levels, and ultimately face the final boss.

Game Features

  • Multiple weapon types including red, blue, and white weapons, plus a special area attack ability
  • Progressive difficulty system with six distinct stages of enemy spawning
  • Experience-based progression system using collectible rats that enhance player abilities
  • Three distinct enemy types with unique characteristics and behaviors
  • A final boss battle featuring special attacks and complex game mechanics
