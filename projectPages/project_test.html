About the Project

Order Up! is a fast-paced card game where players compete as chefs to complete recipes using ingredient cards. The game features simultaneous play mechanics, creating an exciting rush to complete recipes before other players.

Game Mechanics

The core gameplay revolves around matching ingredient cards to recipe requirements. Players start with 7 ingredient cards and compete to complete the currently revealed recipe.

  • Wildcard "Fully Stocked Fridge" cards that can substitute any ingredient
  • Penalty cards that force discards or recipe forfeitures
  • Dynamic hand management system
  • Simultaneous play format

Development Process

Initial Version

First iteration included trading mechanics and strict hand limits, which proved to slow down gameplay.

Refinements

Through iterative testing, several key improvements were made to enhance gameplay flow and balance.

Final Balance

Careful adjustment of card quantities and mechanics to achieve optimal gameplay experience.
