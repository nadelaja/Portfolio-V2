A Night At Home

Interactive Experience / 2024 / Lead Designer & Developer

Project Overview

A first-person exploration game that transforms ordinary household objects into extraordinary adventures.

Duration: 3 months
Team Size: 3 people
Role: Lead Designer

About the Project

"A Night At Home" is an atmospheric first-person exploration game that transforms ordinary household objects into extraordinary adventures. Players navigate through a seemingly normal house where everyday items hold mysterious stories and unexpected challenges.

The Challenge

The main challenge was creating an engaging experience within the confined space of a house, while maintaining a sense of wonder and discovery. We needed to make familiar objects feel new and interesting, without losing their fundamental recognizability.

The Solution

We developed a unique interaction system that allows players to examine and manipulate objects in unexpected ways. Each room contains its own self-contained puzzle sequence, with objects that transform both visually and functionally as players progress.
