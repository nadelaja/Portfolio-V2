About the Project

"A Night At Home" is an atmospheric first-person exploration game that transforms ordinary household objects into extraordinary adventures. Players navigate through a seemingly normal house where everyday items hold mysterious stories and unexpected challenges.

The Challenge

The main challenge was creating an engaging experience within the confined space of a house, while maintaining a sense of wonder and discovery. We needed to make familiar objects feel new and interesting, without losing their fundamental recognizability.

The Solution

We developed a unique interaction system that allows players to examine and manipulate objects in unexpected ways. Each room contains its own self-contained puzzle sequence, with objects that transform both visually and functionally as players progress. The art style emphasizes the contrast between the mundane and the magical through subtle visual effects and carefully crafted lighting.

Results & Impact

The game has been well-received by players and critics alike, particularly praised for its innovative approach to domestic spaces and its ability to create wonder from everyday objects. It has been featured in several indie game festivals and maintains a positive user rating on major gaming platforms.
