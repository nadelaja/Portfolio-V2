Hi! I’m Jummy, a designer and creative technologist who loves making playful, accessible, and purposeful experiences. Whether it’s a game, a research tool, or an interface, I enjoy connecting people with ideas through thoughtful interaction and design.

My background blends Information Science, Graphic Design, and Human Computer Interaction, this fuels my approach to UX, storytelling, and systems thinking. I’m especially interested in gamification, digital equity, and speculative design. Let's build weird things that work!

I'm a maker by nature and also love working with my hands, when I am not working on my designs or illustrations, I’m probably reading, shopping for merch, or binge watching something on tv.
